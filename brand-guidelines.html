KPFK Brand Guidelines

Voice of Resistance. Independent. Community-powered.

Quick Reference Cheat Sheet

Mission: Promote peace, social justice, and cultural expression through fearless, independent media.

Core Colors: #c41e3a, #000000, #555555, #ffffff.

Typography: League Spartan for big, bold hero headlines and special show banners only. Helvetica Neue Bold for all website headers and subheads. Helvetica Neue Regular or Open Sans for body text and paragraphs.

Voice: Bold, direct, people-first, no corporate jargon.

Contact: branding@kpfk.org

Logo Usage

Always use approved logos. Don’t stretch, recolor, or overlay. Maintain clear space and correct proportions.

Main Logo: Official materials.
Shepard Fairey Logo: Limited edition collabs only.
Alternates: Events, merch, or special uses.

Color Palette

Red is our hero color for urgency and boldness. Use black and gray for text, white for clean layouts.

#c41e3a (Primary Red)
#000000 (Black)
#555555 (Dark Gray)
#ffffff (White)

Typography

League Spartan should be used selectively for big hero headlines and campaign splash text. Use Helvetica Neue Bold for all site headers and section titles to ensure readability. Body text must always be Helvetica Neue Regular or Open Sans. Never use decorative or script fonts. Example sizes: Hero headline: 48px, subhead: 32px, body: 18px.

Imagery

Show real community action and authentic moments. Never use generic stock photos or staged imagery.

Voice & Tone

Be bold, direct, people-first. Avoid jargon and fluff. Use phrases like “Join the movement,” “Community-powered radio,” “Unfiltered voices.” Avoid “Tune in now,” “Award-winning,” “Broadcasting excellence.” When in doubt, default to urgent, human, empowering language.

Applications

Apply consistently across flyers, social posts, merch, web, on-air visuals. Event flyers: logo top-center or top-left, red background with white or black text. Social tiles: strong base colors, clean overlays. For exceptions, consult branding@kpfk.org.

Brand Violations

Never alter logo colors, stretch or skew logos, add shadows or outlines, or overlay text on logos. Maintain clear space at all times.

Accessibility & Icons

Maintain at least 4.5:1 contrast ratio for normal text, 3:1 for large text. Use overlays or solid backgrounds behind text on images. Icons (favicon/app): use simplified mark only, no text.

Version & Contact
